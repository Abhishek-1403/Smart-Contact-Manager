Welcome to Abhisheka's Enterprises "Smart Contact manager" Application..

Now no need to worry for manage your contacts, The smart contact manager provide the whole solution for saving and managing your contacts.
You can save your contacts in just few easy steps:
Step 1: Create your user account using the signup option in top right of application.
Step 2: Login to application by Using your " Email " as username and Password.
Step 3: Now you can use "Add new contact" button on dashboard page or "Add Contact " tab in sidebar for start adding the contacts.
Step 4: You can see your contacts in "view contacts" tab in sidebar.
Password Recovery Process:
Step 1: Go to login page and select forgot password link.
Step 2: Enter your Ragisterd Email and send the OTP to Email.
Step 3: Enter the OTP and Verify the same.
Step 4: Enter new Password and login again.
Manage Contact and Account:
Info: We provide the opportunity to user for update their Account details and Contacts details even after the creation.
Account: You can update your Account through the update button present in "Profile " tab in sidebar.
Contacts: And Contacts in "view contacts" tab in sidebar or in the contact profile page by clicking the email of contact in "view Contacts " tab.
Remember: Use the random name of image before uploading as it's required for managing the multiple images.
